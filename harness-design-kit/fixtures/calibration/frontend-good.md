# Frontend Good Example

## Context
Premium product landing page with a strong visual identity.

## What Good Looks Like
- Distinct typography and composition.
- Clear hierarchy without generic card grids.
- Functionality remains obvious on mobile and desktop.

## Expected Evaluation
- design quality: high
- originality: high
- craft: high
- functionality: high

## Why This Matters
- Use this as a positive anchor when the evaluator becomes too lenient toward generic work.
